Catch the Perfect Wave

Mancora is home to some of the most consistent and fun waves in Peru. Whether it's your first time or you're a seasoned pro, we have something for you.

Surf Lessons & Coaching

Our experienced, local instructors are passionate about surfing and sharing their knowledge. We focus on safety, fun, and progression.

  • Beginner Lessons: Learn the basics of paddling, popping up, and wave etiquette in a safe, fun environment.
  • Intermediate Coaching: Ready to turn? We'll help you improve your technique, read waves, and start carving.
  • Advanced Guiding: Let our local guides take you to the best, uncrowded breaks in the region based on the day's swell and tide.

Lessons and rentals available as add-ons when booking your stay.

Board Rentals

Travel light and use our extensive quiver. We have boards for all levels and conditions, from high-performance shortboards to funboards and longboards.

  • - Soft-top foam boards (perfect for beginners)
  • - Funboards & Mini-Mals (7'0" - 8'0")
  • - Longboards (9'0" +)
  • - Performance Shortboards (various sizes)

Rentals available by the hour, day, or week as add-ons during booking.

Ride the Wind

Mancora is a world-renowned kitesurfing spot, thanks to our consistent thermal winds and warm waters. Come see why kiters return here year after year.

IKO Certified Lessons

Our kite school is IKO certified, ensuring the highest standards of safety and teaching quality. We offer courses for all levels.

  • Discovery (3 Hours): Learn the basics, safety, and fly a trainer kite on the beach.
  • Full Course (9 Hours): Get in the water! This course will take you from body-dragging to your first water starts.
  • Advanced Coaching: Perfect your transitions, learn to jump, or dial in your unhooked tricks.

IKO courses available as add-ons when booking your stay.

The Spot: Conditions & Rentals

The Wind

Consistent side-onshore thermal winds. Best season is April to December. Average 18-25 knots, perfect for 7m-10m kites.

The Water

Warm water all year - no wetsuit needed! A mix of flat water inside the reef and fun waves further out. Wide, sandy beach for setup.

Gear & Storage

We rent the latest Duotone & Core kites and boards. Secure storage available. Gear rental can be added when booking.

The Eco House Philosophy

"Eco House" isn't just a nameit's our promise. We are committed to sustainable tourism that respects our beautiful environment and supports our local community.

Eco-Construction

Our buildings are made from locally sourced wood, bamboo, and recycled materials. The design promotes natural airflow, reducing the need for A/C.

Water Conservation

We capture and treat 100% of our greywater, which is then used to irrigate our lush tropical gardens. All fixtures are low-flow to reduce waste.

Renewable Energy

Solar panels heat all of our water, and we utilize LED lighting and energy-efficient appliances throughout the property to minimize our footprint.

How to Get Here

We are located just north of Mancora town, in the peaceful Las Pocitas beach area. We are happy to arrange airport transfers.

  • By Air: The closest airports are Talara (TAL - 1.5 hours), Tumbes (TBP - 2 hours), and Piura (PIU - 3 hours). All accept daily flights from Lima (LIM).
  • By Bus: Luxury buses run daily from Lima, Guayaquil (Ecuador), and other major cities in Peru.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to visit?

For **surfing**, swell is consistent year-round (April-Nov for bigger waves). For **kitesurfing**, the wind season is May to December. For pure sunshine, December-March is hottest.

How do I book surf/kite packages?

Easy! First, book your accommodation using the "Book Now" button. During the checkout process in our booking engine (Guesty), you will see all available surf lessons, kite courses, and gear rentals listed as optional "add-ons" or "extras" that you can add to your reservation before completing the payment.

How do I get from the airport?

We can arrange a trusted private taxi to pick you up from Talara (TAL), Tumbes (TBP), or Piura (PIU) airports. Please contact us with your flight details after booking to coordinate your transfer. Rates vary depending on the airport.

Is breakfast included?

Yes! A delicious, healthy breakfast featuring local fruits, granola, eggs, fresh juices, and Peruvian coffee is included for all guests staying with us.
